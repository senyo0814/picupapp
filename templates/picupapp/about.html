About MyPicUpApp

MyPicUpApp is a user-friendly web platform built for uploading, managing, and mapping photos with location awareness and smart visibility options.

Key Features

  • GPS and timestamp extraction from uploaded photos
  • Interactive map showing photo locations
  • Share photos publicly, privately, or with groups
  • Organize uploads into photo groups
  • Filter and search by location, user, group, or date
  • Email notifications to users or groups when new uploads are made

Whether you're documenting field data, coordinating team uploads, or sharing geo-tagged photos, MyPicUpApp provides a streamlined experience built on Django, PostgreSQL, and Google Cloud Storage.
